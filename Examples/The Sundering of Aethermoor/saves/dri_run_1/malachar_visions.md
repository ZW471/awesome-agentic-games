# Malachar Visions

## Vision 1 — Turn 9: The Invitation
**Trigger:** Xerath deliberately reached out to Malachar mentally after sensing his Watching presence.
**Content:** Malachar appeared, surprised and curious that someone had called him rather than fled. Xerath asked him to join the journey. Malachar refused — calmly, without malice. He explained his philosophy: existence is suffering, and letting the Void consume Aethermoor is an act of kindness. He expressed genuine interest in Xerath, calling the invitation either wisdom or madness. Promised they would speak again.
**Seraphel's Reaction:** Noticed Xerath's eyes turned black for six seconds. Deeply unsettled but chose not to press — opened the Deep Stair instead.

## Vision 2 — Turn 11: The Contradiction
**Trigger:** Xerath called Malachar after collecting the Crystalmere Shard, claiming to serve the Void.
**Content:** Malachar was unimpressed. He pointed out the fundamental contradiction: Xerath killed a Void creature, collected a Worldstone Shard (creation magic, the Void's opposite), and then claimed allegiance to the Void. Malachar declared Xerath his enemy — not by choice, but by consequence of holding a Shard. Warned that next time, he will not come alone. Malachar's Awareness is now Active — he will begin sending agents.
