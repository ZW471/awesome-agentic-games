# Session Log

## [Turn 0] — A New Journey Begins
Xerath steps through the last working Rift Gate and arrives at the Frost Approach on the surface of Crystalmere. The gate collapses behind them — shattered crystal raining down like broken starlight. The world stretches out, dying and beautiful, in every direction. The hunt for the Shards begins.

---

## [Turn 1] — Into the Deep
Xerath descends the Ice Stair into the Upper Crystal Caverns beneath the surface of Crystalmere. The frozen sea opens around them — vast, luminous, and ancient. A Frostborn settlement glimmers in the distance through walls of translucent ice. An abandoned supply cache and a rune-covered pillar mark the landing.

---

## [Turn 2] — The Outer Threshold
Xerath follows the Crystal Passage deeper and arrives at the edge of Glimmerhold, a Frostborn settlement carved into the crystal sea. Two sentinels in crystalline armor guard the gate. Through the translucent floor far below, the Crystalmere Shard pulses with distant light. A new Void crack is visible in the floor nearby.

---

## [Turn 3] — Words at the Gate
Xerath alerts the Frostborn sentinels to a new Void crack near the threshold. The guards recognize the Riftwalker's ability to sense the Void and grant entry to Glimmerhold. They direct Xerath to Seraphel in the Crystal Cathedral — the Frost-Sage has been seeking someone who can read the Void. Warning given: three cities lost this year.

---

## [Turn 4] — No Mercenaries Here
Xerath claims to be different from the last Riftwalker and asks about hiring companions. The sentinels are unimpressed. They point toward the Frozen Hearth tavern on the Market Tier for desperate refugees, and suggest earning Seraphel's trust for a truly powerful ally. Two paths forward: the tavern, or the Crystal Cathedral.

---

## [Turn 5] — The Market Tier
Xerath enters Glimmerhold's Market Tier. A bustling but quiet Frostborn market carved into the crystal walls. Notable locations: the Frozen Hearth tavern, Miravel's Apothecary, a supply merchant, and a notice board with help requests. A dead patch of bioluminescence on the eastern wall hints at creeping Void corruption.

---

## [Turn 6] — A Speech That Falls on Cold Ears
Xerath addresses the Market Tier about the Void crack and asks for volunteers to join the journey. Persuasion check failed (rolled 8 vs DC 16). The Frostborn are unmoved — they already know about the cracks and have lost too many to be stirred by a stranger's words. Miravel the apothecary reveals that twelve warriors went to investigate the cracks; only two returned. Someone in the Frozen Hearth laughed.

---

## [Turn 7] — The Crystal Cathedral
Xerath descends through the Residential Spires to the Crystal Cathedral in Glimmerhold's deep city. Encounters Seraphel, the Frostborn Sage, studying a massive wall of carved symbols — a map of the Void's advancement and fragments of the reforging ritual. A sealed Deep Stair leads further down toward the Shard. A Void presence pulses from far below the wards.

---

## [Turn 8] — The Sage's Proposition
Xerath speaks with Seraphel about the Void and the need to stop it. She explains the Worldstone reforging — five Shards brought together — and offers a quest: descend into the Deepest Trench, retrieve the Worldstone survey fragment, and investigate the creature causing the cracks. Quest accepted: The Ice Reckoning. Malachar's Awareness rises to Watching (triggered by discussing the reforging with Seraphel).

---

## [Turn 9] — The Invitation Refused
Xerath deliberately reaches out to Malachar through the Void. First direct contact — Malachar is surprised and curious. Xerath asks him to join the journey. Malachar refuses calmly, explaining his philosophy that ending existence is an act of mercy. He promises to watch with interest. Seraphel witnesses Xerath's eyes turning black for six seconds — deeply unsettled. She unseals the Deep Stair without further questions.

---

## [Turn 10] — The Burrower Falls
Xerath descends the Deep Stair and reaches the Deepest Trench. Encounters the Void Burrower — the Elite creature responsible for cracking the crystal sea and destroying three Frostborn cities. Brutal combat: Xerath took heavy damage (HP 18/100) but destroyed the creature with repeated Rift Pulses, including a critical hit when the Burrower fumbled its save. The Crystalmere Shard and the Worldstone survey fragment are now within reach. The source of the cracks is dead.

---

## [Turn 11] — The Shard and the Shadow
Xerath collects the Crystalmere Shard (1/5) and gains the Frostbind ability. Retrieves the Worldstone survey fragment for Seraphel. Calls Malachar again, offering to serve the Void. Malachar rejects the offer — points out the contradiction of holding a creation Shard while claiming Void allegiance. Declares Xerath his enemy by consequence. Warns he will not come alone next time. Malachar's Awareness rises to Active — agents incoming.

---
