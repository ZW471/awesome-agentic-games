# Current Location

**Realm:** Thornwood
**Area:** Forest Border
**Zone:** The Ridgeback Trail

## Description
The path climbs steadily along the forest's eastern edge, where the treeline thins just enough to let the grey sky show through. Massive roots knot across the trail like the knuckles of buried hands. To your left, the forest drops away into deep green shadow. To your right, the land rises toward a rocky ridge crowned with gnarled, wind-bent trees. Ahead, the trail widens into what looks like a clearing — and from it, the unmistakable smell of woodsmoke.

## Exits & Paths
- **The clearing ahead (north):** The trail opens into a broader space. Smoke rises from somewhere within. Sounds of activity — voices, the creak of rope.
- **Down the slope (west):** A steep descent into the deeper forest. The canopy closes completely below.
- **Back along the forest edge (south):** Returns to the First Canopy edge where you arrived.

## Points of Interest
- **Woodsmoke:** Drifting from the clearing ahead. Where there is fire, there are people — or at least, there were.
- **Claw marks on the trees:** Three parallel gouges scored deep into the bark of a massive oak beside the trail. Recent. Something large passed through here.
- **A scrap of cloth:** Caught on a thorn-bush at the trail's edge. Dark green fabric, torn. It looks like part of a scout's cloak.
