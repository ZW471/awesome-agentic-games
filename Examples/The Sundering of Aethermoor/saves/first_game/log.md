# Session Log

## [Turn 0] — A New Journey Begins
Lobster steps through the last working Rift Gate and arrives at Thornwood's forest border — the First Canopy. The gate collapses behind them, its pale stone cracking and going dark. The world stretches out, dying and beautiful, in every direction. The hunt for the Shards begins.

---

## [Turn 1] — The Ridgeback Trail
Lobster follows the eastern path along the forest edge, climbing toward higher ground. The trail rises steadily, revealing claw marks on the trees and a scrap of torn scout's cloak. Ahead, woodsmoke and voices drift from a clearing. Something large has passed through here recently.
