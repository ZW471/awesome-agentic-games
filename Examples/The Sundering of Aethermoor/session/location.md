# Current Location

**Realm:** Crystalmere
**Area:** The Deepest Trench

## Description
The trench floor is a ruin of cracked crystal and fading Void-stain where the Burrower's body dissolved. The air still crackles with residual Rift energy. Ahead, at the far end of the trench, the Crystalmere Shard blazes inside its cathedral of flash-frozen ice — a fist-sized crystal burning with pale white light so intense it casts hard shadows across the entire chamber. At the base of the ice cathedral, a crystal tablet leans against the frozen wall: the Worldstone survey fragment, left by an ancient expedition. The Shard's pulse is deafening to your Rift Sense — warm, steady, alive, calling to you.
