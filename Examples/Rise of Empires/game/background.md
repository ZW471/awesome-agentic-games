# Background — Rise of Empires / 帝国崛起

## Setting / 设定

The world of **Terra Nova** is a vast continent surrounded by uncharted seas. Multiple civilizations have emerged from the dawn of history, each with unique strengths, cultures, and ambitions. From humble villages, these civilizations will grow — through diplomacy, warfare, trade, and innovation — to shape the fate of the world.

**新世大陆**是一片被未知海洋环绕的广袤大陆。多个文明从历史的黎明中崛起，各有独特的优势、文化和野心。从简陋的村落开始，这些文明将通过外交、战争、贸易和创新成长壮大，塑造世界的命运。

## Eras / 时代

The game spans five eras of civilization:

| Era | Name (EN) | Name (CN) | Turns | Key Features |
|-----|-----------|-----------|-------|--------------|
| I | Ancient | 远古时代 | 1-20 | Agriculture, writing, bronze working |
| II | Classical | 古典时代 | 21-45 | Philosophy, iron, early empires |
| III | Medieval | 中世纪 | 46-75 | Feudalism, religion, gunpowder |
| IV | Renaissance | 文艺复兴 | 76-110 | Exploration, printing, science |
| V | Modern | 近现代 | 111-150 | Industry, democracy, global trade |

## Tone / 叙事风格

- **Grand and epic** — the player is shaping history.
- **Strategic with narrative flair** — decisions feel weighty, outcomes are described vividly.
- **Moments of personality** — advisors, rival leaders, and events add color.
- Present information clearly with tables and structured formats for easy scanning.
- Use the player's chosen language consistently throughout the session.

## Victory Conditions / 胜利条件

A civilization can achieve victory through any of these paths:

1. **Domination / 征服胜利** — Control 70%+ of the map's territories.
2. **Cultural / 文化胜利** — Accumulate 500 Culture points and build the World Wonder "Grand Exhibition".
3. **Scientific / 科技胜利** — Research all technologies and complete the "Space Program" project.
4. **Diplomatic / 外交胜利** — Achieve "Alliance" status with 3+ civilizations simultaneously.
5. **Score / 分数胜利** — Highest total score when Turn 150 is reached.

## Score Calculation / 分数计算

Score is calculated from multiple factors:
- Population: 1 point per 2 population
- Territories: 3 points per territory controlled
- Technologies: 5 points per technology researched
- Culture: 1 point per culture point
- Wonders: 20 points per wonder built
- Military victories: 5 points per battle won
- Trade agreements: 3 points per active trade route
